MAE 3780 Final Project Robot Competition.

Our team, consisting of myself, Sean Dempsey, and Maddie Vercher, worked together to design a robot for Cornell's Cube Craze tournament as a part of our Mechatronics class. We opted for a unique defensive approach of a deployable lattice wall that blocked the other team from collecting cubes.

Throughout the project, I contributed heavily to the robots mechanical design and fabrication, alongside assisting with assembly as we achieved milestones along the way. For the design pitch and report, I was in charge of creating the flowcharts, sketches, and circuit diagram. I was also instrumental in the competiton, as I was always the one to balance the wall behind our robot. See the video to the right for a snippet of our robot in action during the competition!
